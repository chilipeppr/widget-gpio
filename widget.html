Spacing (XYZ)
Calibration will take a heat measurement of the object at each position in the X, Y, and Z axis at the spacing point. You should place a known heat source like a soldering iron tip at the center. Then, if your calibration of the infrared heat sensor is correct, you should get a consistent heat reading of the heat source when directly overhead at each Z layer. If you dont't, then your Field of View of the heat sensor is too broad.
